Strategic Catalyst Announced Late 2025

North American Entry: CanadaOne Acquisition

Eagers has announced a definitive agreement to acquire a 65% stake in CanadaOne Auto for AUD $1.04 billion. This move marks a structural shift from a domestic ANZ player to a global automotive retailer.

Strategic Rationale

  • 1

    Scale & Geography

    Instant entry into a G7 market with a 42-dealership network. Canada's market dynamics mirror Australia's (fragmented, high margin potential).

  • 2

    The "easyauto123" Export

    Plan to overlay the proven 'easyauto123' fixed-price used car model onto CanadaOne's existing footprint, leveraging the inventory of 42 sites.

  • 3

    Mitsubishi Corp (MC) Partnership

    MC holds a 20% stake in the easyauto123/Carlins vehicle. This partnership provides capital support and strategic global logistics for the expansion.
